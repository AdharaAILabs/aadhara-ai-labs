Our Expertise

Deep Learning

Our deep learning expertise spans across computer vision, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning. We design and implement custom neural network architectures tailored to your specific needs.

  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for image recognition and processing
  • Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) and Transformers for sequence modeling
  • Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) for content generation
  • Transfer learning and fine-tuning pre-trained models
  • Model optimization for deployment on resource-constrained devices

Machine Learning

We leverage traditional machine learning algorithms and techniques to solve complex problems where deep learning might not be the optimal approach. Our expertise covers the full machine learning pipeline.

  • Supervised learning for classification and regression tasks
  • Unsupervised learning for clustering and dimensionality reduction
  • Feature engineering and selection
  • Ensemble methods for improved performance
  • Anomaly detection and time series analysis

LLM Fine-Tuning

We specialize in adapting large language models to specific domains and tasks through fine-tuning techniques. Our approach ensures optimal performance while minimizing computational resources.

  • Parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) methods like LoRA and QLoRA
  • Instruction fine-tuning for improved task performance
  • Domain adaptation for specialized knowledge areas
  • Alignment techniques for safer and more helpful AI systems
  • Quantization and optimization for deployment

Agentic AI

We build autonomous AI agents that can perform complex tasks with minimal human intervention. Our agentic systems combine planning, reasoning, and tool use capabilities to solve real-world problems.

  • Multi-agent systems for collaborative problem-solving
  • Tool-using agents that leverage external APIs and services
  • Planning and reasoning frameworks for complex task execution
  • Memory systems for improved context retention
  • Human-in-the-loop designs for oversight and guidance

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Our RAG systems enhance language models with external knowledge retrieval, enabling more accurate, up-to-date, and verifiable responses. We design end-to-end RAG pipelines tailored to your specific use case.

  • Document processing and chunking strategies
  • Vector database selection and optimization
  • Advanced embedding techniques for improved retrieval
  • Query reformulation and expansion
  • Hybrid search combining semantic and keyword-based approaches
